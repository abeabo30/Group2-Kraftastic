DIY Simple Cat/Dog Toy

by: Anthony Marsh

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • Empty toilet paper rolls or cardboard tubes
  • Scissors
  • Optional: Catnip (for cats)

A DIY simple cat/dog toy is a quick and easy project that provides hours of entertainment for your furry friend. This toy is perfect for cats who love to chase and pounce or dogs who enjoy batting and tossing. Made from common household items, it's an affordable way to keep your pet engaged and stimulated. Whether you have a playful cat or an energetic dog, this DIY toy is sure to be a hit!

STEP 1: Gather Materials Collect

Gather Materials Collect empty toilet paper rolls or cardboard tubes. Ensure they are clean and free from any residual materials.

STEP 2: Cut Tubes Using

Cut Tubes Using scissors, cut the cardboard tubes into smaller segments. You can make them as long or short as you like, depending on your pet's preferences and the size of the tubes.

STEP 3: Optional: Add Catnip (for Cats)

Optional: Add Catnip (for Cats) If you're making the toy for your cat, you can add a pinch of catnip inside each cardboard segment. Catnip is a natural herb that many cats find irresistible and can enhance their enjoyment of the toy.

STEP 4: Playtime Once

Playtime Once you've prepared the cardboard segments, simply scatter them on the floor or toss them to your pet. Cats will enjoy batting them around, while dogs may enjoy chasing after them. You can also roll the segments across the floor for your pet to chase and fetch.

STEP 5: Supervise Your Pet

Supervise Your Pet While this DIY toy is simple and safe, always supervise your pet during playtime to ensure they don't ingest any small pieces of cardboard. Replace the cardboard segments if they become damaged or worn to prevent choking hazards.

STEP 6: Store and Reuse

Store and Reuse After playtime, you can store the cardboard segments in a container or bag for future use. They can be reused multiple times, making this DIY toy a sustainable and cost-effective option for keeping your pet entertained.
