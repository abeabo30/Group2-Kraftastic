DIY Vintage Frames

Pressing flowers and leaves is a fun way to preserve a piece of your garden. I think many of us experimented with pressing flowers in a heavy book as children. It’s kind of magical to watch a flower transform from fresh and three-dimensional to dry, flat and papery thin with only some pressure and time.

Upcycled Furniture

Give old furniture a new lease on life by upcycling it with a fresh coat of paint, new hardware, or decorative techniques like decoupage or stenciling. For example, transform an outdated dresser into a stylish statement piece for your bedroom or breathe new life into a tired coffee table with a mosaic tile top.

Add a bohemian touch to your home with handmade macramé plant hangers. Learn basic macramé knots and techniques to create beautiful hangers for your favorite indoor plants. Hang them near windows or in unused corners to bring greenery into your space while adding texture and visual interest
