Khan Academy

by: Sal Khan

Khan Academy is a nonprofit educational platform that offers a wide range of instructional videos, practice exercises, and educational resources across various subjects, including math, science, economics, humanities, and more. The platform aims to provide free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere, empowering learners of all ages to master concepts at their own pace through personalized learning experiences.

Supplies Needed:

  • Computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone
  • Internet connection
  • Note-taking tools (e.g., pen and paper, digital note-taking apps)
  • Headphones (optional, for video lectures)

Step 1: Explore Subject Areas

Visit the Khan Academy website or download the mobile app to explore the available subject areas and topics. Khan Academy covers a wide range of subjects, from core academic disciplines to specialized topics and test preparation resources.

Step 2: Select a Subject or Topic

Choose a subject or topic that aligns with your learning goals or interests. Browse through the available content within the selected subject area to find specific lessons or tutorials that you want to explore.

Step 3: Access Video Lessons

Watch instructional videos or tutorials on the chosen topic. Khan Academy offers a vast library of video lessons taught by expert educators, covering fundamental concepts, problem-solving strategies, and real-world applications.

Step 4: Complete Practice Exercises

After watching a video lesson, reinforce your understanding by completing practice exercises or problems related to the topic. Khan Academy provides interactive practice exercises with instant feedback to help you assess your mastery of the material.

Step 5: Track Progress

Monitor your progress and performance by tracking your completion of video lessons and practice exercises. Khan Academy's progress tracking features allow you to see which topics you've mastered and identify areas where you may need additional practice.

Step 6: Take Notes and Review

Take notes while watching video lessons or working through practice exercises to capture key concepts, formulas, and strategies. Review your notes regularly to reinforce your learning and deepen your understanding of the material.

Step 7: Engage with Community

Participate in the Khan Academy community by asking questions, sharing insights, and interacting with fellow learners. Khan Academy's discussion forums provide a platform for learners to support each other, exchange ideas, and collaborate on learning projects.

Step 8: Explore Advanced Topics

Once you've mastered foundational concepts, explore more advanced topics or subjects within Khan Academy's extensive library. Challenge yourself to delve deeper into areas of interest or pursue new learning opportunities to expand your knowledge and skills.

Step 9: Prepare for Standardized Tests

Use Khan Academy's test preparation resources to prepare for standardized exams such as the SAT, ACT, GMAT, or LSAT. Khan Academy offers comprehensive study plans, practice tests, and personalized recommendations to help you achieve your target scores.

Step 10: Review Feedback and Improve

Pay attention to feedback provided by Khan Academy's interactive exercises and assessments. Use feedback to identify areas for improvement, address misconceptions, and refine your problem-solving skills.

Step 11: Share Progress and Achievements

Celebrate your progress and achievements by sharing milestones and accomplishments with friends, family, or peers. Khan Academy allows you to share badges, certificates, and progress reports to showcase your learning journey and accomplishments.

Step 12: Continue Learning

Continuously explore new subjects, topics, and learning opportunities on Khan Academy to fuel your curiosity and passion for lifelong learning. Khan Academy's accessible and engaging resources make it easy to pursue your educational goals and expand your knowledge across diverse disciplines.
