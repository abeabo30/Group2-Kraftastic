DIY Photo Collage

by: Thegraciefarmer

TOOLS NEEDED:

  • Foam brushes
  • Ruler
  • Scissors
  • Brayer (optional)

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • Photos printed with a laserjet printer
  • Mod Podge Gloss
  • 20 × 30 foam core boards - two, black
  • Poster hangers
  • Double sided tape
  • Duck Tape
  • Toothpicks

This photo craft is such a great way to capture family members pics for moms, but it’s also a fun craft for teens. They will love making a huge display of photos of their friends! This looks great in a living room or a dorm room.

Step 1:

Plan out the photos you would like to use. Each board holds 24 photos (sized 4" square) in a 4 x 6 grid.

Step 2:

Print out your images in 4x4" squares. Cut them out using the scissors. Lay them out on the foam core boards.

Step 3:

Pick up each photo, apply a layer of Mod Podge to the board, and smooth down the photo.

Step 4:

Rolling out with a brayer is preferable. Repeat until all the photos are applied to the board. Let dry for 15 - 20 minutes.

Step 5:

Apply a top coat over all the photos and the board using Mod Podge. Let dry and then apply a second coat.

Step 6:

Let dry overnight before hanging with Command Strips (or the method of your choice).
