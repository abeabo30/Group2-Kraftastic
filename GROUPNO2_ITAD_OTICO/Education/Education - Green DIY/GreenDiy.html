The Green DIY

The Green DIY education fosters a deeper understanding of ecological principles while instilling practical skills. By integrating sustainability into the learning process, participants not only acquire knowledge but also develop a sense of responsibility towards the planet.

Upcycled Learning Tools

In the realm of eco-conscious education, the concept of "The Green DIY" shines as a beacon of sustainability. Embracing the ethos of reduce, reuse, and recycle, this approach encourages learners to repurpose old materials creatively, transforming them into innovative learning aids and organizers.

Eco-friendly Learning Activities

If you’ve caught even one news story about the environment lately, you know just how important environmental activities for students can be. The saying “children are our future” is used so often that it can begin to sound cliched, but let’s face it: Truer words are rarely spoken. The students learning about nature, climate change, and sustainability in your classroom today will be the stewards of the environment in 10 or 20 years. The future of our planet will soon be in their hands. As a teacher, you’re making an important impact on our future too.

Citizen Science Projects

NASA’s citizen science projects are collaborations between scientists and interested members of the public. Through these collaborations, volunteers (known as citizen scientists) have helped make thousands of important scientific discoveries. More than 519 NASA citizen scientists have been named as co-authors on refereed scientific publications.
