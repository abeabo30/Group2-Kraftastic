DIY Confetti Coasters

by: CraftKlatch

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • Tiles
  • Mod Podge Dishwasher Safe
  • Tissue Paper
  • Scissors
  • A paint brush
  • Felt

Learn how to make DIY Confetti Coasters using old materials, help me reuse this things and make it a beautiful design.

Step 1:

 Start by lightly cleaning your tiles with rubbing alcohol. Cut up pieces of tissue paper into little squares. You could also use a circle punch or cut triangles or rectangles or really anything you can come up with. Five piles of colored tissue paper squares, a pair of scissors, and tiles

Step 2:

 Cover your tile with a medium coat of Mod Podge. Place the pieces of tissue paper directly onto the tiles. I had fun making different color schemes for different coasters. I let some of the squares fold over the edges. You may need to dab a little extra Mod Podge on the edges when you fold them over.

Step 3:

Let this first coat dry before you add another one! I learned this the hard way. When I tried to add a second coat too soon, it smeared a little bit of the tissue paper’s dye into the Mod Podge.

Step 4:

When I waited to add a second coat, this really didn’t happen at all.

Step 5:

Applying tissue paper squares to the tile using Mod Podge.

Step 6:

Optional: You may even want to add a third coat after the second coat has completely dried. Add felt circles or a big piece of felt to the bottoms of the tiles so that it won’t scratch your furniture, and enjoy!
