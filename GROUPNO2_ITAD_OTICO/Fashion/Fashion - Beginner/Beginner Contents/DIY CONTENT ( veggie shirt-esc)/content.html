Veggie Shirt

by: Instructables

The Veggie Shirt is a vibrant and playful tie-dye creation inspired by the colorful hues of fresh vegetables. Its design features a kaleidoscope of shades reminiscent of nature's bounty, including rich greens, deep purples, bright yellows, and perhaps even hints of orange and red. The pattern evokes the lively energy of a bustling farmer's market, with swirling and intertwining colors that dance across the fabric like vines in a garden.

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • White Shirt
  • Vegatables
  • Paints or Dye
  • Paint Brush

STEP 1:

Prepare all the things needed

STEP 2:

Take the Plain White Shirt

STEP 3:

Cut some of the vegetables

STEP 4:

Then you can use the colors/paints.

STEP 5:

Dip the veggies into the paints.

STEP 6:

You can paint with brushes to make some designs.

STEP 7:

Wait until it dries.
