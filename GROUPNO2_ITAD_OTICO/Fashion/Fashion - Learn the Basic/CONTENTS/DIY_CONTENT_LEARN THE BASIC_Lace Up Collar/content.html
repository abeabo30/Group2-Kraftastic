Lace Up Collar

by: Thuy Sewing

A Lace-Up Collar infuses a classic garment with a touch of contemporary flair, creating a stylish focal point that draws attention to the neckline. This design features a traditional collar adorned with a series of delicate lace-up details, adding a hint of intrigue and sophistication to any outfit. Crafted from high-quality fabric, the Lace-Up Collar shirt offers a perfect balance between elegance and modernity.

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • Shirt or top with a collar
  • Eyelets/grommets
  • Eyelet/grommet tool kit (including a punch and setter)
  • Fabric marker or chalk
  • Scissors
  • Eyelet/grommet setting anvil (optional)
  • Hammer
  • Lace or ribbon

Step 1: Choose Your Shirt

Select a shirt or top with a collar that you'd like to embellish with a lace-up collar. A button-up shirt or blouse works well for this DIY.

Step 2: Plan Your Design

Decide where you want the lace-up collar to be on your shirt. You can lace up the collar from the neckline to the top button, or you can choose a shorter section for a more subtle look. Use a fabric marker or chalk to lightly mark the placement of the eyelets/grommets along the collar.

Step 3: Mark and Punch Holes

Using the fabric marker or chalk, mark evenly spaced points along the collar where you want the eyelets/grommets to be placed. These will be the holes through which you'll lace the ribbon or lace.

Use the punch tool from your eyelet/grommet tool kit to punch holes at each marked point. Make sure the holes are large enough to accommodate the eyelets/grommets.

Step 4: Install Eyelets/Grommets

Following the instructions provided with your eyelet/grommet tool kit, insert the eyelets/grommets into each punched hole. Place the eyelet/grommet on the setting anvil (if using) with the smooth side facing up. Position the hole over the eyelet/grommet, then use the setter tool and hammer to secure it in place.

Repeat this process for each punched hole along the collar until all eyelets/grommets are installed.

Step 5: Lace Up the Collar

Cut a length of lace or ribbon long enough to lace through all the eyelets/grommets along the collar with some extra length for tying. Starting at one end of the collar, lace the ribbon or lace through each eyelet/grommet, alternating between top and bottom until you reach the other end.

Step 6: Tie Off the Ends

Once you've laced the ribbon or lace through all the eyelets/grommets, tie off the ends in a bow or knot at the top of the collar. You can leave the ends long for a dramatic look or trim them for a more polished finish.

Step 7: Adjust and Secure

Adjust the tightness of the laces to your liking, ensuring that the collar lays flat and comfortable against your neck. If desired, you can add a drop of fabric glue or clear nail polish to the knots to secure them in place.

Step 8: Try on and Style

Try on your shirt with the newly embellished lace-up collar and style it with your favorite bottoms and accessories. Enjoy wearing your customized creation with pride!
