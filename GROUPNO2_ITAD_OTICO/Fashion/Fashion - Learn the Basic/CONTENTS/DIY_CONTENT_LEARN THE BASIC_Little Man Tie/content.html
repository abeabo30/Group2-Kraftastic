Little Man Tie

by: Charmed by Ashley

A little man tie is a fun and rewarding DIY project that allows you to customize the fabric, style, and size to suit your child's personality and preferences. Experiment with different fabrics and patterns to create a unique and personalized accessory that your little one will love to wear.

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • Fabric of your choice (cotton, silk, or other lightweight fabric)
  • Interfacing (optional, for added structure)
  • Coordinating thread
  • Sewing machine or needle and thread
  • Fabric scissors or rotary cutter and mat
  • Ruler or measuring tape
  • Iron and ironing board
  • Tie pattern (you can create your own or find one online)

Step 1: Choose Fabric and Cut

Select your fabric for the tie. Lightweight cotton or silk fabrics work well for little man ties. Pre-wash and press your fabric to remove any wrinkles and shrinkage. Then, use fabric scissors or a rotary cutter and mat to cut out your tie pieces according to your chosen pattern.

Step 2: Cut Interfacing (Optional)

If you're using interfacing to add structure to your tie, cut out interfacing pieces using the same pattern as your fabric pieces. You'll need one interfacing piece for the main tie and one for the lining.

Step 3: Prepare Interfacing

Following the manufacturer's instructions, fuse the interfacing pieces to the wrong side of the corresponding fabric pieces using an iron. This will help the tie maintain its shape and hold its form.

Step 4: Sew Tie Pieces Together

With right sides facing each other, place the main tie fabric piece on top of the lining fabric piece. Pin around the edges to hold them in place. Sew around the edges, leaving the bottom edge open for turning. Use a Â¼ inch seam allowance and backstitch at the beginning and end of each seam.

Step 5: Trim Excess Fabric

Trim any excess fabric from the corners and edges of the tie, being careful not to cut into the stitches. This will reduce bulk and help the tie lay flat once turned right side out.

Step 6: Turn Tie Right Side Out

Carefully turn the tie right side out through the open bottom edge. Use a turning tool or the blunt end of a pencil to push out the corners and smooth the edges. Press the tie with an iron to create crisp, clean lines.

Step 7: Fold and Press

Fold the open bottom edge of the tie inward and press with an iron to create a clean edge. Use a ladder stitch or slip stitch to hand sew the opening closed.

Step 8: Shape and Press

Shape the tie by folding it in half lengthwise and pressing with an iron to create a crease down the center. Fold the sides of the tie inward towards the center crease and press again to create the classic tie shape.

Step 9: Finish and Enjoy

Your little man tie is now complete and ready to be worn! Pair it with a button-down shirt and dress pants for a dapper look, or add it to a casual outfit for a touch of charm. Enjoy seeing your little one looking stylish and adorable in their handmade tie!
