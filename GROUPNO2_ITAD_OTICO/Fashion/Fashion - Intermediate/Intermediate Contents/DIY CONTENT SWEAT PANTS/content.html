DIY Pants to Sweatpants

by: Creative Fashion Blog

This DIY project guides you through transforming ordinary long pants into cozy sweatpants. Inspired by the desire for affordable grey sweats, this tutorial offers a solution by repurposing oversized grey track pants into stylish sweatpants with tailored hems.

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • Regular pants (ensure they have a bottom hem width of at least 2cm; for example, cott on Nike ones)
  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread of corresponding color
  • Elastic banding
  • Tape and bobby pin or safety pin
  • Ruler or measuring tool
  • Pencil (for marking measurements, ideal for perfectionists)
  • Sweatpants to use as a guide
  • Hot glue (not recommended, but optional for those seeking an alternative to sewing; note that sewing yields better results)


STEP 1: Cutting

Rest assured, minimal cutting is required for this step. Begin by turning the bottom edge of the pants inside out. Locate a vertical seam inside the bottom of the pants leg and carefully snip it open, aiming to cut as little as possible. The goal is to create a hollow space along the entire edge to accommodate the elastic.

Step 2: Inserting the Elastic Band

To insert the elastic band, cut a piece of elastic to your desired length, ensuring it's loose enough to comfortably wrap around your ankle. Attach a small but sturdy object, like a bobby pin or safety pin, to one end of the elastic to aid in pulling it through the opening. Optionally, sew the extra length of elastic to the opening of the hem to prevent it from retracting into the fabric, making it easier to handle.

Step 3: Sewing Ends Together

Now that the elastic is successfully pulled through, locate the extra bit of ending you sewed onto the beginning of the tunnel. Remove the bobby pin and carefully sew the ends of the elastic together, ensuring they're not twisted. If there's enough thread remaining, continue sewing the rest of the hem together until it's fully closed. Repeat the entire process on the other pants leg to complete the transformation into sweatpants.

STEP 4: Finish!

Congratulations, you've completed your DIY sweatpants transformation! This project is perfect for those dull indoor days and when you're in need of a comfy pair of sweats. Remember, you can never have too many sweatpants! I hope this guide was helpful, and enjoy rocking your new cozy creation!
